Sangria

Description

Sangria is an alcoholic punch that originated in Spain and Portugal. There are two ommon varieties: red sangria and white sangria. It's typically made with wine, liqueur, sparkling water, fruit juice, and lots of sliced fruit. When you need a crowd-pleasing cocktail, sangria is the obvious choice. A pitcher of sangria is the perfect summer drink to sip on at your backyard barbecue or party. So, let us introduce you to the best sangria recipe on the internet. It's easy to make and, even better, it's irresistibly delicious. Today we will be making red sangria!

Ingredients

  • 1 (750 milliliter) bottle dry red wine
  • ½ cup brandy
  • ½ cup triple sec
  • ⅓ cup frozen lemonade concentrate
  • ⅓ cup orange juice
  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • ¼ cup white sugar (Optional
  • 1 medium orange, sliced into rounds
  • 1 medium lemon, sliced into rounds
  • 1 medium lime, sliced into rounds
  • 8 maraschino cherries
  • 2 cups carbonated water (Optional)
